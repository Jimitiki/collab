ColLab

ColLab is a website for collaborators designed to help users get things done. By creating projects on this site, users are asking for your help with their projects. And it doesn't just have to be monetary - users can offer resources, time, expertise and more. Get started by setting up your own project to receive help or browse existing projects to help fellow users.
